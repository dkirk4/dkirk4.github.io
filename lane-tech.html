LT Cradles to Crayons

A school club that I am part of is Lane Tech's Cradles to Crayons Club. Cradles to crayons is an organization that helps children from birth to age 12 who are living in homeless situations or low-income homes gain the supplies they need to help survive at school, at home, and at play. Through Lane Tech's Cradles to Crayons club I am able to help volunteer to help kids get the necessities that they need.
